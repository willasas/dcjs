4. 正则表达式参考

数字相关
NUMBER: /^-?[0-9]+$/
NON_ZERO_POSITIVE_INTEGER: /^[1-9]\d*$/
NUMBER_WITH_ONE_TO_TWO_DECIMALS: /^(\-)?\d+(\.\d{1,2})?$/
字符相关
CHINESE_CHARACTERS: /^[\u4e00-\u9fa5]{0,}$/
ENGLISH_LETTERS: /^[A-Za-z]+$/
NUMBERS_AND_ENGLISH_LETTERS: /^[A-Za-z0-9]+$/
常用格式
EMAIL: /^\w+([-+.]\w+)*@\w+([-.]\w+)*\.\w+([-.]\w+)*$/
CHINA_MOBILE_PHONE: /^(13[0-9]|14[5|7]|15[0|1|2|3|5|6|7|8|9]|18[0|1|2|3|5|6|7|8|9])\d{8}$/
ID_CARD_NUMBER: /^\d{15}|\d{18}$/
INTERNET_URL: /[a-zA-Z]+:\/\/[^\s]*/
